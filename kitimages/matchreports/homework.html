Sat 29th September 2018-Premier League

Chelsea 1-1 Liverpool

Hazard(23)    Sturridge(89)

Liverpool striker Daniel Sturridge scored a magnifcent goal late on to deny Chelsea a win against Liverpool for the second time in four days.It was a game where the two sides shared the points.It was the first of his touches after coming on as a substitute as he lashed the ball from outside the box into the top right corner to make it well out of Chelsea's goalkeeper Kepa's reach.Liverpool provided Chelsea with their sternest test so far as they had won their first six league games of the season and were two points above Chelsea heading into the game. Many changes were made from the game against Liverpool at Wednesday in the EFL Cup but Chelsea boss stuck with the same lineup that drew with West Ham last weekend.On a tense night as this was a top of the table clash.In a game where there was an abundance of chances at both ends it was a juicy encounter between the two sides.The first chance came to Liverpool only in the second minute where Alexander-Arnold's ball deflected of Kante and fell to Salah but his low drive was comfortably saved by Kepa.Liverpool were getting the chances but ex Chelsea player Salah again fired a shot well wide an shortly after Sdio Mane hit a shot well over after Firmino teed him up. The twentieth minute was approaching and a slick move from Hazard and Kovacic allowed Hazard to get away from the Liverpool defence and he drove a shot into the net to score his seventh goal of the season.
