Sat 24th November 2018-Premier League

Tottenham 3-1 Chelsea

Alli(7) Kane(16) Son Heung-Min(54)       Giroud(85)

Tottenham outplayed Chelsea as Chelsea lost there first game of the 2018/19 season.Tottenham started of with a high intensity and pressing game and Chelsea were finding it very difficult to play Sarri ball.Most would say it was Chelsea's worst performance of the season and as Sarri mentioned a few days ago that Chelsea have not started matches well recently this was another performance to emphasise the fact that we had been playing poor in the opening stages of matches.Sarri named an unchanged lineup from the previous 0-0 draw against Everton despite Kovacic recovering from a minor injury sustained during the international break.We were made to pay for a sloppy start where Alli headed in a powerful Eriksen free kick and Harry Kane who was England's hero during the international break was given too much room to shoot from twenty five yards.Tottenham added a third goal nine minutes after the interval through a swift counter attack as Alli played a pass through to Son who drove past Jorginho and David Luiz to then put the slot the ball past Kepa.From then on it was an uphill task but Giroud did pull one back with five minutes to play through Azpi's delivery but unfortunatley the damage was done.

Tottenham deserved to win hwever when it was 1-0 Tottenham defender Juan Foyth clumsily pushed Hazard in the penalty area and things might've have been different if a penalty was given.Some people Kante should've been playing in a more defensive position rather than offensive and play their instead of Jorginho as Kante is faster and is arguably the best cdm in the world.Chelsea will look to recover on Thursday as they play PAOK in the Europa League and then Fulham next Sunday in the premier league.
